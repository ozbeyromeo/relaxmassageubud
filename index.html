Welcome to Relax Massage Ubud

Located in the heart of Ubud, we offer authentic Balinese massage and therapeutic treatments. Our experienced therapists provide a range of services designed to rejuvenate your body and mind.

Whether you're looking for deep tissue massage, traditional Balinese techniques, or aromatherapy, we ensure a peaceful and professional environment for your relaxation journey.

Our Services

Hot Stones

Experience the warmth and soothing relief of the Tropics as therapist glides the smooth stone around the body. Stimulating circulation to remove blockages and opens the energy flow throughout your body.

Aromatherapy

Indulge yourself in an individually tailored combination of aromatherapy and Balinese massage for your perfect personalized treatment, enhancing emotional well-being and body rejuvenation.

Deep Tissue

A therapeutic full body massage focusing on the back, neck and shoulder, relieving chronic patterns of tension and stress, soothe sore muscles and restore energy flow.

Reflexology

A relaxing therapy that applies pressure to specific points on the feet, improving overall health, balancing energy, and enhancing relaxation throughout the body.

Balinese

Traditional Balinese massage using a combination of gentle stretches, acupressure, and aromatherapy oils to stimulate the flow of blood, oxygen, and energy around your body.
